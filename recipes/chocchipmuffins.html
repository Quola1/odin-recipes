Chocolate Chip Muffins

A recipe for chocolate goodness!

It include creamy, delicious chocolates with a hint of Vanilla to bring out the freshness of the muffins.

These will be great for family gatherings for either breakfast or dessert but can also be made for anytime your having a sweet craving!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Sugars
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients in one bowl and the dry ingredients in another.
  2. Make a well in the dry mixture, then pour in the wet mixture and stir.
  3. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin. Top with sugar.
  4. Bake the muffins until the top springs back when lightly pressed.
